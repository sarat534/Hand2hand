About us

Hello! Welcome to Hand2Hand. This website
is created to help students  with settling in the
city. Our team  is  driven  by  the passion   of
being able  to help  international students like
you. Here you can find all the stuff  you want.
From books to blenders, we offers  everything
at student friendly prices. You know the  cool
thing!! This website is only for the students of
BTH. So you  can make  new friends either by
selling  or buying. We  hope  that  you find our 
site useful. Don't forget to have fun!

If  you  have any queries, please contact  our
team via email.

If you encounter any issues please report them
using our issues forum. This will help us serve
you in a better way.
